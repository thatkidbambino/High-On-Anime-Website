Meet the Team

Host · Matthew

Matthew is the current editor of High On Anime. At 24 years old, he has a passion for anime and strives to grow the channel to new heights. Out of all the anime that Matt has watched, Naruto remains his favorite show of all time. But Matthew hopes to find a show that will overcome Naruto, and become his favorite show of all time.

Co-Host · RJ

RJ is the anime guru of High On Anime. At 21 years old, RJ has established a deep bond in the anime culture and has extensive knowledge with many shows. Currently, his favorite anime show is Bleach. However, his opinion on shows tend to change over time, as his other favorites are Jujutsu Kaisen and Dragon Ball.

Co-Host · Eric

Eric is the newcomer to anime on the High On Anime team. While Eric remains to have a dislike for the shows, he has found to appreciate the art style and music to a select few anime. While he continues his search for a show that will captivate him, he remains to be the 'Non-Anime Fan' of the channel.
